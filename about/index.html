About Us - Option Chain Analyzer

Our Mission

The Option Chain Analyzer is built with a simple objective: to transform highly dense, static spreadsheet datasets from the National Stock Exchange of India (NSE) into human-friendly, high-probability visualizations. We believe options data should be transparent and accessible to retail analysts.

Visual Support and Resistance Methodology

Derivatives analysis is often challenging when viewing standard, colorless option tables. Our analyzer parses real-time Open Interest (OI) and Traded Volume values, calculating Put-to-Call (PCR) and Call-to-Put (CPR) ratios strike-by-strike. When an outstanding contract multiply parameter of 6.0x or higher is detected, the platform automatically color-codes that level to highlight prominent defense zones (Green for support, Red for resistance).

Confluence Over Execution

We recommend using this analyzer as a supportive, corroborative filter in conjunction with your primary price charting tools. Check your candlesticks, verify your trendlines, and use our color-mapped highlights to see if they align with high-probability support or resistance levels.
